Logs of liquors

You can log your favorite liquors with Chidori. Take photo of liquor, and input a little information. That's all. You can look back your logs with beautiful list.

Shops

You don't need to remember the shops anymore. Chidori remembers the location of shops which have your favorite liquors.

Chidori has powerful search and sort methods. You can find your logs easily, and sort every logs.
